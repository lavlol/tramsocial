what's this transit page all about?

on this page you will find transit map proposals/ideas, images, and other cool stuff related to transit in the valley of the sun :)

clicking on the image will take you to a google maps plan for the system

a proposal for an expanded rail network for the phoenix valley

this proposal includes current light rail, peoplemover, and streetcar service, in addition to new light rail, peoplemover, streetcar, and regional rail service. we affectionately named the proposed regional rail system 'valtrain' based off the former (failed) proposal for a metro system for phoenix in the late 80s (it was called valtrans).

a proposal for a night train network in the american southwest

this proposal includes night train services that would span the southwest of the usa. los angeles and phoenix would serve as its main service hubs, with certain trains having one or two overnight stops. the idea is that you could fall asleep on a train in say, phoenix and wake up in el paso or los angeles. how cool would that be!?
